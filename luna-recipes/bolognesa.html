Chocolate lava cake

The final plate presentation

Chocolate lava cake is the perfect dessert for fodies

You just need a few ingredients and some of time.

ingredients

  • Butter 75gr.
  • Chocolate 115gr.
  • Sugar 75gr.
  • Flour 75gr.
  • Eggs 4u.

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate with the butter in the microwave.
  2. Mix the eggs with the sugar.
  3. Mix the chocolate and butter with the eggs and sugar, then stir the flour.
  4. Divide batter evenly between the prepared ramekins.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the sides are firm but centers are still soft, approx 8 to 10 minutes. Allow to rest for 1 minute, then carefully invert the ramekins on dessert plates and dust powdered sugar. Serve immediately.
