Goulash with spaetzle

the final plate presentation

Goulash is a popular meal in Central Europe, in of hungarian origin. It's simple recipe, so it's perfect for winter days.

You just need a few ingredients and quite a bit of time.

ingredients

For te goulash

  • Roast beef 1kg.
  • White onions 1kg.
  • Red wine 2 cups approx.
  • Tomatoe paste one tablespoon
  • Paprika two tablespoons
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

For the spaetzle

  • Milk 100ml.
  • Soda 100ml. approx
  • Eggs 4u.
  • Flour 400gr.
  • Baking powder one teaspoon
  • Nutmeg one teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps:

Goulash

  1. You need to prepare your ingredients first. So you have to cut the meat into cubes, and the onions into julienne strips.
  2. Then you have to seal the meat in a pot, when it's done, add the cuted onion and the red wine.
  3. Once the onion it's transparent add the tomatoe past and all the spices.
  4. If it's needed add some water and cover. It needs to be at list 3 or 4 hours on minimum fire. You can go tasting flavor and correcting.

Spaetzle

  1. By first, mix the wet ingredients.
  2. Then add the dry ones.
  3. Once you have the dough, take it to the fridge for half an hour.
  4. Finally boil the spaetzle with help of the spaetzle machine and serve with the goulash.
