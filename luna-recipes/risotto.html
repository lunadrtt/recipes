Risotto

The final plate presentation

Risotto is a popular meal from Italy. It's a nice creamy way to cook rice.

You just need a few ingredients and some time.

ingredients

  • Carnaroli rice 100ml.
  • Vegatables broth
  • Onion 1u.
  • Butter one tablespoon
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Turmeric to taste

Steps:

  1. By first, chop the onion and fry it in a pan.
  2. When it's transparent, add the rice.
  3. Once the rice is color nacre, add some broth an stir it.
  4. Stir every 3 minutes until the rice is ready and then add the butter and cheese.
